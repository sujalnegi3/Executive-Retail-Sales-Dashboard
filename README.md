📊 Executive Retail Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Executive Retail Sales Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence dashboard built using **Microsoft Power BI**. It provides a comprehensive overview of retail sales performance by tracking key business metrics such as revenue, profit, profit margin, orders, regional performance, and product category insights.

Designed for executives and business stakeholders, this dashboard enables quick, data-driven decision-making through intuitive visualizations and KPI tracking.

---

## 🚀 Dashboard Highlights

### 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- 💰 Total Revenue: **2.51 Billion**
- 📊 Total Profit: **375.53 Million**
- 📉 Profit Margin: **14.97%**
- 📦 Total Orders: **100K**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Revenue Trend Analysis across multiple years (2019–2023)
- Monthly Revenue Performance
- Revenue Distribution by Product Category
- Regional Sales Performance
- Executive KPI Cards
- Business Insight Callouts for quick decision-making

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Revenue demonstrates consistent performance across multiple years.
- The **East** region generates the highest overall revenue.
- **Household Items** contribute the largest share of total sales among all product categories.
- The business maintains a healthy **14.97% profit margin**, indicating strong profitability.
- Executive KPI cards provide a high-level summary of overall business performance.
